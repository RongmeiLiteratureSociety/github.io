🏛️ About RLS

The Rongmei Literature Society (RLS) is a registered organization founded to preserve and promote the Rongmei language — a rich and complex tonal language of the Naga people of Northeast India.

Our work includes linguistic documentation, native audio recordings, phonetic transcription, and open-access digital archives for global scholars and communities.

📌 Objectives

  • Develop a comprehensive dictionary with tone-aware transcription
  • Support native speakers and youth in language learning
  • Encourage community storytelling and oral tradition
  • Seek academic and international recognition

👥 Who We Are

RLS is made up of linguists, elders, cultural leaders, and young volunteers across Rongmei-speaking regions. We also collaborate with researchers and institutions focused on endangered languages.
