🤝 How You Can Contribute

We welcome contributions from everyone passionate about preserving the Rongmei language. Whether you’re a native speaker, a linguist, developer, or student — you can help.

📖 Add Words

Help expand our dictionary by submitting new Rongmei words with meanings, usage, and examples.

🎙️ Record Audio

Native speakers can contribute recordings to help illustrate the tonal pronunciation of each word.

💻 Code & Tools

Develop scripts for tone markup, search, or visualization to support language learners and researchers.

🌍 Translate

Translate website content or dictionary entries into English or other languages for international access.

📦 How to Submit

Email your contributions to rongmeilit@gmail.com or open a pull request on our GitHub repository.
